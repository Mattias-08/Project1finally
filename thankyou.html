Lund Cathedral (Domkyrkan) is the seat of the Lutheran Diocese of Lund in southern Sweden. It is the oldest and largest church in Sweden, dating back to the 12th century and known for its distinctive Romanesque architecture. Explore its rich history, admire its awe-inspiring architecture, and experience the beauty of this landmark cathedral.
